The New Instagram Algorithm

In the past, instagram like most social media companies with a feed of content, used chronological order for their posts. However, after some extensive research they discovered that with that sort of algorithm, users inevitably miss some of their favorite accounts' content. Now instagram has created an algorithm for their feed that is largely driven by engagement. So now, regardless of what time you check your account, you will see the content of those you engage with the most, as opposed to those who posted most recently. The update has been met with backlash, but instagram hasn't relented at all.

Capitalizing on Instagram's New Article

To take advantage of instagram's latest algorithm, you have to engage with your followers. In the past, influencers often posted their content and left followers to wonder. But now if they do, they risk their posts not being shown to the right people, at the right times, as intended. You must encourage more dialogue with your followers, and giving them call to actions to like, comment, and share. Many are also pushing followers to turn on post notifications to guarantee engagement. Additionally, you must engage with your followers on their content as well, to encourage them to do the same.

6 Ways to Boost Engagement with your Instagram Content

6 Ways to boost your engagement are as follows: find relevant accounts to follow and follow back, post regularly but test different times, go through photos on niche hashtags and like some, come with creative content ideas based on what people are posting in niche hashtags, add niche hashtags to your photos a little while after you've uploaded them for a second boost, try new things with your photography. Too many are trying to grow by aiming for the stars and their missing the opportunities in smaller communities. If you leverage local networks to grow your account, you'll be surprised.

How to Get More Followers the Right Way

Across all social media channels, the hunt is on for followers. Some may cheat and purchase them, others will use spam accounts. However, there is a right way to do it. On twitter for example, you should conduct regular follower audits to unfollow unnecessary accounts and follow back good ones, participate in chats or threads to gain exposure and engagement, leverage the power of social listening to anticipate trends, follow first to encourage others to follow you back, copy industry leaders to see what the effective strategies are, and engage with your followers at a human level to understand them.

Leveraging Feature Accounts for Massive Exposure

Feature accounts are accounts that are devoted to some niche topic/content and feature other instagram users. Since these accounts are focused on a specific niche, many gain large followings within their respective fields. If an instagrammer is featured on one of these accounts, they have the potential to grow their followings astronomically. So how do you get there? Here are a few things you can do: have a consistent feed, improve our photography skills, study other instagrammers that get featured, and post your content at the right time. If you do this, you stand a better chance of winning.
