Our MIssion

Social media is changing the world that we live in. Everything from how we entertain ourselves, to how we take care of our basic necessities. Inadvertently, social media has created new ways for orgnaizations to connect with consumers. Our goal is to redefine the marketing experience by taking advantage of how people use social media.

However, since this is such a new space, we encountered a problem: there is so little relevant research on how small businesses and individuals can leverage social media to grow their brands, attract new customers, and increase conversions. So this blog is intended to be a catalog of strategies, actionable tips, and everything else you need to know to make the most of your social media presence.

Why Read Our Blog?

Actionable Insights

We're not going to sell you any "Like4Like" program, or tell you to use a million and one hashtags. We focus on relevant and actionable social media strategies that connect with people.

We Practice What We Preach

Not only do we have past experience helping small business leearage social media effectively -- from leading web development on sites with 500k+ hits, to using social media to grow a business from 0 to 6 continents and 30 countries -- but we are actively applying the strategies we recommend on our personal accounts and analyzing the results.

Full Commitment

The world is changing and we want to be at the vanguard of these changes. We are committed to continual improvement, learning, and to you. We're willing to invest the sweat equity to master the science and the art of social media for our own benefit, and we're just as committed to sharing everything we learn. So anything you stumble across, you should know is genuine.
