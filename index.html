About the  Author

Humble Beginnings

Originally from Senegal, West Africa but grew up in Greater Washington, D.C. My early days were defined by overcoming bullying and learning how to understand and connect with others. This ignited my passion for the Arts.

Growing Pains

Fast Forward to my early adulthood. I found myself at Howard University, where I study Computer Information Systems and Economics. At Howard, I've made mistakes but I've learned more than I could ever imagine. Like the importance of education, the value of service, and how business can change the world.

Looking Ahead

With my knowledge and experiences in hand, I plan to fulfill my life's purpose and enrich the lives of others. My goal is to leverage the power of technology to inspire myself and others to be our best selves.
